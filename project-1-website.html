About

This project is my first computer tech project ever. I have really enjoyed making it and am proud of what I have managed to do and learn this term. I made many key decisions while creating this website and learnt a lot about how much work really goes into making websites. Turns out that a lot of work goes into websites and a lot of thought with me constantly having to decide colours, fonts and borders that all match. As well as learning how to add background photos and how to choose ones that won't be extremely pixelated and multiply a million times. One of the biggest challenges I face was creating the staircase effect on the homepage of this website. I tried everything I could think of - a lot of which would have worked if I hadn't made the mistake of forgetting one div. In the future I want to make this site more creative and interactive. In the future I also want to create even more projects that I will hopefully find equally challenging and equally entertaining as this one.
